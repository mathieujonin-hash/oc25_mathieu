Knightmare "A Voice in the Dark"




Hi! I'm happy to present my wiki about the game I'm currently working on. It's called Knightmare "A Voice in the Dark" and it's my first game project ever.

It started as a school project called “Travail de maturité” (Graduation Project). and since I couldn't finish it completely at the time I only created the “functional” part of the game. That is, everything the player can do while playing.

Please note that every character, ennemy and NPCs presented in this Wiki are only ideas I wanted to add in the game. They're not really doing anything for now...

If you are interested about seeing what every beings I imagined was supposed to look like feel free to explore this wiki.










This wiki and the game it is related to are still under development. Content can change.
