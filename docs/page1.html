The ennemies and bosses

A corrupted elf supposed to attack you in the forest zone

The Mage is a mini boss fight that you can encounter twice in the game, he is floating around and launching spells at the player

The Golem is a major boss, he is big, strong and deals a lot of damage if he touches you

He starts glowing when you trigger his second phase and starts attacking faster

The Nightmare King is the last boss of the game, he is the main villain in the story

His second phase should not be taken lightly as he is much faster and deals more damage
