The Player


Well, here you are... Don't you recognize yourself? That's right, this is the player's appearance. You are supposed to be followed by a will-o'-the-wisp all the way from the beggining and later discover that it is actually your spirit.


The NPCs


That's the old man who guides you through the first steps in the game. He tells you a bit about what happend in the kingdom and gives you some clues about what to do next.

The ennemies and bosses

The corrupted elf comes to you when you enter the forest. At first, he acts almost kindly but as you discover that he works for the Nigthmare King, he begins to attack you.

The Mage is a mini boss fight that you can encounter twice in the game, he is floating around and launching spells at the player.

The Golem is a major boss, he is big, strong and deals a lot of damage if he touches you.

He starts glowing when you trigger his second phase and starts attacking faster.

The Nightmare King is the last boss of the game, he is the main villain in the story.

His second phase should not be taken lightly as he is much faster and deals more damage.
